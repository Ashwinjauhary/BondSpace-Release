Features

Everything your relationship needs

One app to chat, play, share, grow, and love — privately and securely.

🔐
End-to-End Encrypted Chat
Messages encrypted with libsodium NaCl. Voice notes, image sharing, reactions, pinned messages, and disappearing messages.
📍
Real-Time Location Sharing
Live GPS sharing on Leaflet maps with mutual consent. "Reached home" alerts, battery tracking, and location history.
🎮
20+ Love-Themed Games
Truth or Dare, Who Knows Me Better, Love Bingo, Memory Match, Story Builder, and 15 more relationship mini-games.
🌳
Bond Tree & Timeline
A living visual timeline of your relationship milestones. Attach photos, set dates, and watch your tree grow.
🤖
Love Guru AI
Your private AI relationship advisor for advice, conflict resolution, date ideas, and a full relationship health score.
🏆
Gamification & Love Arena
Earn XP, level up your bond, and unlock custom sticker packs, profile frames, themes, and emoji packs in the Arena store.
💌
Digital Love Letters
Write time-locked encrypted letters. Set them to unlock on anniversaries, birthdays, or any custom date.
🖼️
Shared Gallery
Album templates (First Meet, Trips, Food, Custom) powered by Cloudinary CDN for photos and videos.
🌍
Community Spaces
Anonymous 1-on-1 chats and group communities — Coding Couples, Long Distance Love, Marriage Planning, and more.
